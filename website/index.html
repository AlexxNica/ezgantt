View working demo and starter spreadsheet.

To make your own:

  1. Copy the spreadsheet
  2. Edit it, following the rules in the notes - especially the date and dependency rules! Don't share it with me. And you MUST keep the word 'gantt' in the worksheet name!
  3. View your Gantt Chart at https://ezgantt.appspot.com/#YOUR_SPREADSHEET_KEY (where the key is https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/[this part is the key]/edit Don't forget the "#" hash! Better still, use the "ezGantt" menu in the spreadsheet.
